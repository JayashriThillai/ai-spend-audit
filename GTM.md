# Go-To-Market Strategy

## Product Overview

AI Spend Audit helps users identify unnecessary AI subscription spending and discover lower-cost alternatives or plan optimizations.


## Target Audience

### Primary Users

- Students using multiple AI tools
- Freelancers paying for separate AI subscriptions
- Small startup teams
- Developers experimenting with multiple coding assistants


### Secondary Users

- Educational institutions
- Small businesses
- Teams with overlapping AI subscriptions


## User Problem

Many users:
- subscribe impulsively to AI tools
- forget recurring payments
- pay for overlapping functionality
- do not track yearly AI spending

The product solves this by:
- auditing spending
- recommending cheaper alternatives
- estimating possible savings


## Positioning

The product is positioned as:

A lightweight AI subscription cost optimizer for individuals and small teams.


## Distribution Strategy

### Initial Channels

- LinkedIn posts
- Student developer communities
- Reddit productivity communities
- Twitter/X developer audience
- College tech groups


### Product-Led Growth

The product encourages sharing because:
- audit reports have shareable URLs
- savings results create curiosity
- users compare AI spending with peers


## Monetization Ideas

Potential future monetization:
- premium team analytics
- organization dashboards
- enterprise audit reports
- subscription tracking integrations
- advanced optimization recommendations


## Success Metrics

Key metrics:
- number of audits generated
- estimated savings generated
- repeat usage
- report shares
- conversion rate


## Competitive Advantage

Advantages include:
- simple UX
- fast audit generation
- lightweight workflow
- shareable reports
- focus on AI subscription optimization specifically


## Future Expansion

Possible future features:
- bank/payment integration
- automatic subscription detection
- AI usage analytics
- yearly trend dashboards
- enterprise cost optimization
