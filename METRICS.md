# Product Metrics

## Core Metrics Tracked

### Total Audits Generated

Measures:
- total usage of the platform
- engagement with audit functionality

Importance:
Shows whether users find the audit process useful.


### Average Monthly Spend

Measures:
- average AI subscription spending among users

Importance:
Helps identify spending patterns and target customer segments.


### Estimated Savings Generated

Measures:
- total estimated savings recommended by the platform

Importance:
Directly demonstrates product value.


### Most Common AI Tools

Measures:
- most frequently audited tools

Importance:
Helps prioritize future integrations and optimization features.


### Conversion Rate

Measures:
- percentage of visitors who generate audits

Importance:
Shows effectiveness of landing page and onboarding experience.


### Return Users

Measures:
- users returning to generate multiple audits

Importance:
Indicates long-term product usefulness.
